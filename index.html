About Us
Gunjan Saxena’s main objective is to create eco-friendly products made from natural materials that cause no harm to the environment. She personally crafts a variety of items including shoe bags, files, bottle bags, shagun envelopes, tote bags, file folders made of cloth, jute planters, jewelry from old cloth, key rings, and even electricity cable baskets. Her initiative demonstrates a commitment to psychology-based principles by utilizing inexpensive, non-polluting materials that are safe for nature. So far, over 500 people have joined Gunjan Saxena in her mission. Her team actively visits rural areas to teach these skills, spreading sustainable practices. She has worked across five Indian states—Uttar Pradesh, Haryana, Rajasthan, Uttarakhand, and Himachal Pradesh—helping villagers gain employment through hands-on training.
